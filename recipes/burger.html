Burger

Description

Burger is one of my favorite meals that I love experimenting with. It is very versatile, and anyone can make a burger to their own taste: custom meat, cheese, bun, topping choices to name a few.

The following burger recipe though is my current favorite. It is perfectly balanced, with my top choice of ingredients, portion sizes, and texture. Give it a go!

Ingredients

  • 4 White sesame seed hamburger bun
  • Unsalted butter, softened, for toasting
  • 450g 75% ground beef
  • ½ sweet onion, medium dice
  • 8 slices American cheese
  • 2 medium sized dill pickles, chip-cut
  • Mustard, for sauce
  • Salt and pepper to taste
